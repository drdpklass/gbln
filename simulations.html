Why GBLN Simulations

A rehearsal for the real decision.

Every simulation in the network shares the same instructional core, whatever the school.

🎯

Realistic Scenarios

Every simulation is built from real business situations — not abstract case questions, but decisions with consequences.

✦

AI Mentor Feedback

AURA reviews every run and offers feedback calibrated to your specific decisions, not a generic rubric.

⚡

Consequence-Based Learning

Decisions carry forward — a choice in session one shapes the scenario you face in session three.

🤝

Solo & Team Modes

Run simulations individually or in cohort teams, mirroring how the same decisions get made at work.

Instant Analytics

Every decision is scored in real time, with a breakdown of what drove the outcome.
